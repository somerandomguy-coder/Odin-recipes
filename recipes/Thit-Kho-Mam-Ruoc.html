Thit Kho Mam Ruoc (Pork with shrimp paste)

Description

It was a meal that represented all the simple things in life. With just some meat, lemongrass and shrimp paste, Thit Kho Mam Ruoc became a humble reminder of those values.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Pork Belly
  • Lemongrass
  • Shrimp Paste
  • Chicken Bouillon Powder
  • Chili
  • Sugar
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Red Shallot

Steps

  1. Heat up a wok or pan and cook the meat on medium-high heat with the chicken bouillon powder for 15 minutes or until the pieces become golden and crispy.
  2. Transfer onto a plate for later, keeping the oil in the wok.
  3. Bring the heat to medium and pour the lemongrass, chili, garlic, ginger and shallots. Cook for 2 minutes or until brown.
  4. Add the shrimp paste in and stir until well combined for 3 minutes.
  5. Turn the heat up to high and add the pork back in. Stir everything together for 5 minutes.
  6. Serve everything immediately with a side of freshly sliced cucumbers and hot rice!
